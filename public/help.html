To quickly locate a client, use the Search feature on the Dashboard. Simply enter the client’s first name, last name, or case number in the search bar, and click Search to view matching results.

If you navigate to the Client List, you will see your entire caseload.

To view a client’s demographic information, click on Client Details or simply select the client’s name from the list.

You can easily add a new client to your caseload by using the Add Client button.
